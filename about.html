Get to know Kodex

What is it

Kodex is a learning tool for students by a student. It is a platform for those who want to learn better, grow their expertise, and keep those skills sharp.

Boost your learning

Kodex has bite-sized concepts you can study anywhere. They are created such so you can fit them in anywhere to fill time.You can easily study them while waiting at the grocery store, on the bus, or in killing time anywhere. They are designed so you quickly improve your understanding of programming concepts.

Use it well

Remember your Lambda and ORM notation from University? What is an AVL tree? Some things are easy to forget when you don't practice them. Flashcards are a great way to discover and close those gaps in your knowledge.

Smarter not Harder

Kodex uses a straight-forward progress system to reduce the amount of studying you do over time. This technique, can reduce the amount of studying you do by half. You will learn more with less effort.
